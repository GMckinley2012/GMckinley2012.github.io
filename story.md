# Black Voters Matter: Visualizing Participation and Power

## Executive Summary

This data storytelling project explores the importance of Black voter participation and its connection to political representation, civic power, and equity. The problem examined is how voting patterns reveal broader issues related to access, engagement, and representation in democratic systems. Using Tableau Public, this project presents an interactive dashboard that allows users to explore voting-related data visually rather than through static numbers alone. The method combines data visualization, filtering, and interactive exploration to help identify patterns and relationships within the data. The key insight is that Black voter participation matters because it influences whose voices are represented in elections, policy decisions, and community outcomes.
